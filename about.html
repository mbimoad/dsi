DELTA SYSTECH INDONESIA is a company agent and licensee of DSI Products, Technic and services in Indonesia. The principal is DYWIDAG SYSTEMS INTERNATIONAL GmbH, Munich, Germany (DSI). DSI is the exclusive licensee for all construction systems and techniques developed by DW. The so-called licensee business of DYCKERHOFF & WIDMANN (DW) has a long tradition dating back to 1950. It was based on the sale of know-how developed by DW and was covered within DW by a separate department, the License Department.

In 1979, DW established DSI as an independent organization to handle licensing and promote DYWIDAG systems globally. Today, DSI, owned by Dyckerhoff & Widmann AG, operates internationally through subsidiaries, associated firms, and representatives. DSI GmbH in Munich coordinates group business, with global activities managed from three headquarters: Munich (Europe, Middle East), Hong Kong (Far East), and Chicago-Bolingbrook (USA, Canada, Latin America).

Based On Dywidag

DYWIDAG is Founded in 1865, DYWIDAG is the oldest civil engineering and building contractors in Germany. Our overseas operation started in 1902. We have continues our international activities ever since and executed a multitude of structures in many countries. DYWIDAG developments such as pre-stressing systems, monocoque construction and free cantilever construction methods have had a decisive influence on modern construction and have established the international reputation of our company.

With decades of experience, we possess the expertise to offer our clients tailored solutions across all phases of construction. Close collaboration allows us to develop ideas and ensure efficient and economically viable projects, ultimately enhancing client satisfaction. Our unwavering commitment to quality assurance applies throughout the construction process, from planning to final execution. We provide comprehensive quality management systems, recognized for their excellence within the German construction industry.
